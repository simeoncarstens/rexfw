propose(local_replica, partner_state, partner_energy, params)¶

Calculates a proposal using information from the replica, the exchange partner state and energy, and possibly other information stored in params

Parameters:
  • local_replica (Replica) – the replica which is supposed to propose a state
  • partner_state (depends on your application) – the state of the exchange partner
  • partner_energy (depends on your application) – the exchange partner’s current energy
  • params (AbstractProposerParams) – parameters the proposer may need to calculate a proposal
